About Sonnet.ly

Sonnet.ly is a collaborative data project committed to analysing Shakespeare's sonnets. Though they were published over 400 years ago, this collection of 154 poems has captured the imagination of generations of readers. Like those before us, we marvel at Shakespeare's preternatural ability to express the scope of human feeling and educate us all on the power of poetry.

This online resource is designed to help casual and experienced readers alike to explore the sonnets like never before, through a user-friendly dashboard offering the traditional rendering of each sonnet alongside modern translations, analsyis, videos, and commentary.

We hope you enjoy Sonnet.ly, and if you have any suggestions as to how we can improve, please do contact us at editors@sonnetly.com.
